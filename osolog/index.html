    About

    Mission statement

    Our mission is to help kids and their families to be able to afford playing sports in their communities. We accomplish this with donated sporting equipment that families no longer need or have grown out of. These donations are given to those that are in need of equipment to participate in youth sports.

    Scholarships are also available to help off set the monetary cost for those who want to participate in sports within their community.
